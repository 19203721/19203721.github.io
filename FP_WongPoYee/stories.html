With millions marching to protest fighting for freedoms under Beijing’s rule over the last 100 days, yellow ribbon challenge police excessive use of force, which causes a big contradiction between police and protesters and leads to antithetical. 

Eventually, an insurmountable gulf grew between Jasmine and his father. 

“I try to explain the movement’s goals of a more democratic Hong Kong to him rationally, but he won’t stand by me, he only trusts what his colleague said and scold me,” she said. “It makes me feel powerless, and want to move out.” 

Jasmine describes herself as a moderate—not one on the frontlines. But she thinks the contradiction between her and her dad just like the protesters and police. 

Their arguments fell on deaf ears with her father, Jasmine finally decided to rent a house herself in order to have a space to calm.
